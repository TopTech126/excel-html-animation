The Alima experience

Always do the right thing for our customers and our communites

Our team is given the freedom and encouraged to always do the right thing the right way. Most service organizations force team members to first ask, “Is this work in scope and to be provided to the customer?”. At Alima, we teach our people to ask a different question; “Is the work being requested the right thing to do?”

Even if the work is outside of the defined scope, team members are always authorized to proceed if the customer needs it right away and it’s the right thing to do. We know that when we do the right thing, customers will also do the same in return.
